CONECT 2024

The Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU), Ile-Ife, Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC), Center of Excellence in Telecommunication is proud to announce its 1st International Conference on Telecommunication, themed "Communications, Optimization, Networking, and Emerging Concepts in Telecommunication (CONECT 2024). This groundbreaking event will bring together leading experts, researchers and industry professionals to explore the latest advancements and challenges in the dynamic field of telecommunications. The conference will delve into critical sub-themes, including 5G/6G networks, AI in communication, green communication, cybersecurity, and rural connectivity, with a focus on fostering innovation, addressing security concerns, and promoting sustainable practices in the Nigerian telecom industry. Join us from December 1st to 3rd, 2024, at OAU Ile-Ife, as we shape the future of telecommnications in Nigeria and beyond through paper presentations, keynote speakers, panel discussions, and workshops. Submit your original research and be a part of this.

SUBMISSION AND PUBLICATION GUIDE

We welcome submissions of original, unpublished work in the form of full papers (4-8 pages) or extended abstracts (1-2 pages). Accepted full papers will be presented orally at the conference, while accepted abstracts will be presented as A1 posters. Novel research in all practical areas of subthemes are welcome. Submissions from academia, government, and industry are encouraged. All accepted papers will be included in the CONECT 2024 Conference proceedings. Extended versions of outstanding papers will be considered for publication in the upcoming Ife Journal of Telecommunications and Allied Technology Journal. All submissions must follow the IEEE formating instructions and must be submitted electronically in MS WORD(doc.) format to the email: oau_ncc_conect@oauife.edu.ng A virtual presentation will be arranged for authors who are unable to travel. However, we strongly encourage physical participation.
